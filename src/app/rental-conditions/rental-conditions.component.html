INSURANCE

The insurance is Kasko (CDW, TPL  and TP), which means that it covers all kind of damage including theft.

Insurance does not cover the following:

  • damage to the tires, wheels, windscreen, underside of the car and interior of the car.
  • damage caused when driving under the influence of alcohol, drugs or other intoxicating substances.
  • damage of the vehicle caused when driving without a valid driving license or if the driver is not authorized in Contract.

    The vehicle may only be used on public roadways. The use of the vehicle in the following circumstances is expressly prohibited--The driving of the vehicle on unauthorized or unsurfaced roads, rough roads or roads whose condition is such that they pose a risk of the vehicle being damaged.

POLICE RECORD IS OBLIGATORY FOR ALL TYPES OF INSURANCE

Without police record full amount of damage or theft will be charged to a Renter. (Call 122 to ask for Police assistance)

In case of the accident or any damage  the Renter is obliged to immediately inform Police and DELTA CAR about the situation and follow their instruction.

Police record is obligatory even under circumstances when the Renter was not present at the time when the damage was done.(e.g. the damage done at the parking lot by unknown person).

Police record is obligatory even in case of a scratch on the car.

All interpersonal right and obligation are regulated by the car rental Contract the Renter signs when taking over the vehicle.

Basic insurance (CDW , TPL and TP)
mandatory insurance with damage liability covers the following:

  • CDW insurance (Collision Damage Waiver) - limits the Client’s liability associated with the vehicle damage to the excess level, depending on the vehicle group. The customer pays the amount towards the claim according to Delta Car rates.
  • TP (Theft Protection) - vehicle theft and vehicle parts theft insurance - this waiver protects you towards the claim the same way as the CDW, explained above.

However, this waiver does not apply in case you left the vehicle unlocked, or you left the key inside, or if you allowed a third party to drive the vehicle without alerting Delta Car. For this insurance to be applied, the customer must provide the police record to Delta Car agent. Alternatively, the insurance is invalid, and you become 100% responsible for the damage and costs. For your own protection, please contact Police immediately (by calling 122), even if you haven’t been present at the site nor witnessed the damaging of your rented vehicle.

This insurance is not valid in case you have caused the damage under the influence of alcohol, drugs and substance abuse, or have damaged it on purpose or by irresponsible behavior; in case you have used the wrong type of fuel or allowed the unauthorized individual to conduct it; in case you used it for any kind of testing or for racing purposes, etc.

TPL (Third Party Liability insurance)

The basic insurance does not cover the following:

  • Vehicle tires damage
  • Vehicle rims damage
  • Damage to the lower part of the vehicle
  • Damage to the interior of the vehicle or windows

except when the damage is caused by an accident or a car crash.

The rates include the following:

ADDITIONAL INSURANCE  

         SCDW
Super Collision Damage Waiver

This insurance removes your liability for the vehicle damage - you are not responsible for any damage costs, even if you have caused the damage, BUT only if you provide the police record. However, the SCDW does not cover the interior damage of the vehicle (except if it was caused by a crash - accident), the damage to vehicle frame, wheels or tires, window, for lost or broken car keys, lost vehicle ID documents. Purchasing this insurance you are also benefiting from the PAI insurance.

PAI is the insurance for the driver and passengers in case of death or invalidity resulting from the accident, to the amoount specified by the insurance company coverage of the DELTA CAR vehicle.

ADVICE: Get acquainted with all the insurance packages and your responsibilities in case of damage or vehicle theft. Please bear in mind that police record about the accident/theft is mandatory for the insurance validation (including the SCDW). WITHOUT THE POLICE RECORD, THE CUSTOMER IS ENTIRELY RESPONSIBLE FOR THE DAMAGE COSTS.
